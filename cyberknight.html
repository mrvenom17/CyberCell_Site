ABOUT THE COMPETITION

Get ready to hack for glory! Cybercell Galgotia’s University is hosting an exciting all-India level Capture the Flag (CTF) competition this year. Participants of all skill levels are invited to test their cybersecurity prowess against a variety of challenging puzzles and scenarios. Whether you’re a seasoned security expert or just starting your journey in the field, this CTF is the perfect opportunity to learn, grow, and compete for amazing prizes. So gather your team, sharpen your skills, and prepare to embark on a thrilling adventure into the world of cybersecurity!
